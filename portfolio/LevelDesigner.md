## Level Designer
Level designers create playable environments that guide players, communicate narrative, and stage interactions through layout, props, and lighting. Their work balances pacing and challenge while ensuring the space naturally suggests what players can do, how they move, and how they engage with the worldâ€”all without explicit instructions.

> [!IMPORTANT\]
> ### What Your Portfolio Should Contain
> -   **Block Mesh Levels** (playable layouts showing scale, flow, and pacing)
> -   **Level Flow & Pacing Diagrams** (charts showing encounters, intensity curves, and progression)
> -  **Spatial Storytelling Proof** (annotations, screenshots, or videos showing narrative conveyed through environment)
> - **Interactive Level Prototypes** (playable examples with scripted events, doors, or dynamic elements)
> - **Navigation & Landmarking** (sightlines, color cues, landmarks to guide player movement)
> - **Gameplay Footage / Walkthroughs** showing the level in action
> - **Environmental Storytelling** (small scenes showing narrative through props, lighting, and layout)
