# Sound Engineer
Sound Engineers design and implement audio for games, including sound effects (SFX), ambient audio, dialogue, and dynamic music. They ensure audio supports gameplay, narrative, and player immersion while maintaining technical quality and proper integration into the game engine.

> \[!IMPORTANT\]
> 
> ### What Your Portfolio Should Contain
> -   **Sound Design Reels**: SFX, Foley, and environmental audio demonstrating creativity and clarity.
> -   **Interactive Audio Implementation**: Wwise, FMOD, or other middleware examples showing audio responding to gameplay events.
> -   **Dynamic / Adaptive Music**: Music and audio systems that change based on game states, player actions, or events.
> -   **Voice-Over & Dialogue**: Recorded, processed, and mixed dialogue samples showing clarity and presence.
> -   **Mixing & Mastering Examples**: Demonstrate balance, volume, and spatial placement of sounds.
>     

# Portfolio Deliverables Checklist

Each portfolio piece should show your ability to create, implement, and integrate high-quality audio in a game context.
-   **Required Deliverables:** Must be included for every portfolio piece.
-   **Add-On Deliverables:** Include only if you personally created them (adaptive audio logic, foley recording, dialogue editing, etc.).
-   **Collaboration Note:** Clearly indicate which parts of the audio pipeline were your work.
